Company History

SNS Global Traders is a women-led export enterprise specializing in premium Indian millets for international markets. Established in 2021, the company was founded with a clear mission to connect India's nutrient-rich traditional grains with the growing global demand for clean, responsibly sourced foods.

We work closely with trusted sourcing networks and follow disciplined quality control, structured export processes, and accurate documentation to ensure consistent standards and reliable delivery.

Built on integrity, professionalism, and long-term vision, SNS Global Traders focuses on creating dependable global supply partnerships grounded in quality and trust.

Our Vision

To become a globally trusted exporter of Indian millets by 2030, recognized for quality consistency, ethical trade practices, and dependable performance.

Our Mission

To supply premium and organic Indian millets with assured quality, competitive pricing, timely shipment, transparent documentation, and flexible trade solutions, simplifying sourcing and strengthening buyer supply chains.

Why Indian Millets?

Millets are traditional supergrains that combine strong nutrition, sustainable farming, and rising global demand. They offer a reliable and future-ready solution for modern food markets.

01

Nutrient-Rich Ancient Grain

5,000+ years of cultivation in India; high in fiber, plant protein, minerals, antioxidants, low GI, and naturally gluten-free.

02

Health & Wellness Aligned

Supports blood sugar balance; ideal for gluten-sensitive, clean-label, and plant-based markets.

03

Sustainable & Climate-Resilient

Low water requirement, eco-friendly crops suited for responsible sourcing.

04

Strong Global Supply

Growing worldwide demand backed by India's large-scale, reliable production.
